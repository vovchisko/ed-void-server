About ED-VOID

ED-VOID is a tool designed for explorers. It helps you with scaning stellar boides, navigation, reporting your exploreation data, and get extended body information, based on existing reports.

For now ED-VOID in beta-testing stage. We collecting data, building database structure and working on upcoming data analyzing tools.

How to start

- Register New Pilot.
- Download Client App.
- Go back web-app and enjoy.

Link for downloading ED-VOID client always available in your profile. And your API-key as well.
